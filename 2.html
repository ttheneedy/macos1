About Us

Mateete Comprehensive Seed Secondary School is a community of learners dedicated to academic excellence, creativity, and integrity. Established in 1995, we have consistently provided a nurturing environment for students to grow, explore, and succeed.

Headmaster's Remarks

"At Mateete Comprehensive Seed Secondary School, we believe that every student has unique potential. Our goal is to provide a nurturing environment that fosters creativity, curiosity, and character. We are proud of our academic achievements and even prouder of the compassionate individuals we help shape every day."

– Mr. Ssendagire Hamuza
